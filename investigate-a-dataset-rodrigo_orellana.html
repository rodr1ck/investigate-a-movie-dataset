Conclusions¶

Question 1: We clearly saw that the most popular genre during the period of 1960-2015 was Drama followed by Comedy. Drama was above all most of the time except during a small period in the lat eighties when Comedy took the lead. In the other side we can see that Foreign movies, TV Movie and War did not account much and were mostly at the bottom of the line chart.

Question 2: We wanted to know what properties related to high revenue movies. We chose to use the filed revenue_adj which the revenue adjusted by inflation at 2010. This seemed a fairer parameter because there are movies from other decades and their value is depreciated with time. Some of the parameters we looked at are popularity, runtime, vote_count, budget_adj and vote_average. In the firt exercise we separated popularity in two groups low and high popular movies. Finding out that highly popular movies tend to achive a high revenue_adj as well. Similarly, for runtime, budget_adj and vote_count we found that to a higher runtime, budget_adj and vote_count we achieved higher revenue. In the last excercise we divided vote_average in four categories to see if there was a variation. However, it was similar and showed that to a higher vote_average there was higher revenue_adj. Some limitation of the study is that I only analysed five factors regarding the revenue of movies and there might others properties we left out that correlate or affect the revenue as well.
